# Contact 

## Demo Submissions

Terminal Dusk is always interested in hearing thoughtful, original electronic music that aligns with the label's artistic vision.
Please submit streaming links only. We do not accept downloadable files or email attachments unless we specifically request them.

### Preferred submission formats

- Soundcloud
- Bandcamp
- YouTube
- Google Drive or Dropbox (browser streaming enabled)

### When submitting, please include

- Artist name
- A short introduction or artist bio
- A link to the release (EP or album preferred over individual tracks)
- Links to your Bandcamp, website, or social media (if available)
- A brief note explaining why you feel your music would be a good fit for Terminal Dusk

Please do not send follow-up emails asking whether we have listened to your submission or requesting feedback. If we like your demo, we'll be sure to get in touch.

Thank you for taking the time to share your work. We appreciate your patience and look forward to hearing your music.
