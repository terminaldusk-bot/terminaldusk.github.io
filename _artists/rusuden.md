short_bio: Rusuden is the primary electronic music alias of Pennsylvania-based producer and composer Justin Morgan, a versatile artist known for bridging the gap between classic analog warmth and futuristic digital design. Crafting intricate soundscapes that blend elements of Acid Techno, IDM, Ambient, his solo work focuses on Braindance style acid textures and electro experimental rhythm. Beyond his many solo releases, Justin expands his sonic footprint as one-half of the electro-techno duo ACEAN alongside partner Anthony Green. Recording out of multiple studios over the years and releasing music through Soho Six Records, the duo delivers a distinct blend of gritty Detroit Techno mixed with chilled-out West Coast sounds. Whether performing solo or collaborating, Justin continues to push the boundaries of modern electronic music with his signature sound.
