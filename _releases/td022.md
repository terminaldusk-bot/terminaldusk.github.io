Hatcha Vs.Lost brings you Get Up & Rock, a seriously bass heavy tune complete with fleeting synth leads & a minimal stripped down sound. This one’s meant for the floor, and will become a 2010 classic!

For the flip side Fault drops the bomb with Babylon Warrior featuring Metric Man on vocals. Smooth as silk, classic reggae vibes, intense bass weight, a very deep tune. Essential.

DJ support from DJ Hatcha, Lost , Droid Sector, Boot, Dubtek & many more.
