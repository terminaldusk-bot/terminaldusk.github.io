Vesicle are 2 promising young producers hailing from Finland.

They have had numerous releases with distinguished labels such as Red Volume and our friends at Paradise Lost. For their Terminal Dusk 12″ vinyl release they unleash the ferocious ‘Acid Violence’ with it’s squelchy synth drones, ominous sci-fi samples and thick low end. Equally terrorizing on the floor or the living room.

For the AA side The Bassist steps in and remixes the now classic ‘Streetwise’ from 2009′s TD016 Snow lp.Intricate production, high energy, driving percussion and crushing bass bring this record full circle.

Strictly limited to 300 black vinyl copies for the world.
DJ support from 6blocc, Fault, Roommate, The Subdivision & many others.
Bless.
