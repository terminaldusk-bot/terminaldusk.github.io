Terminal Dusk delivers the 4th various artist compilation in our “color” series. The Snow 12″ is white hot mayhem with 4 tracks sure to blaze up dancefloors worldwide.

The A side serves up a deep roller from Sasquatch featuring Oova on vocals. Funeral delivers with precision programming & a menacing bassline sure to get your head nodding. Rounding out the A side the Finnish duo Vesicle makes their vinyl debut! Streetwise has slick production, punishing bass & haunting overtones, help welcome them to the Terminal Dusk family.

On the flip, The SubDivision takes it to a whole new level with Le Mani Su, a jumpup/dubstep track soon to become an anthem. Enjoy one of the most wobbly basslines we’ve heard, with DJ support from Djunya. Finishing off the record in fine style is Misk with Headcase, an all out dub attack, brutal and infectious with deep grooves.
