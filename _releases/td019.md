TD019 contains a pair of high power collaborations, first up is The Bassist (USA) & Droid Sector (AUS) with Jah is Coming, a seriously dubbed out party tune with extra thick reggae vibes, crushing low end & an unforgettable groove. Massive.

For the AA side, we present Matta (UK) & Dubtek (USA) with Witchcraft, a high tech roller with intricate melodies, deep sweeping bass & dark vibes. This record has recieved DJ support from some big players including Babylon System, Antiserum, DJ Boot, DJ Lukki, along with countless others.
