Terminal Dusk drops the last bomb of 2009 with the 1st 12″ in our new split series.

Terminal Dusk veteran Komonazmuk dominates the A side with VCO. Detroit meets Bristol with a blazing, pulsing, tricked up roller, complete with lush ethereal female vocals, serious bass weight & intricate electro leads.

Next up is Boot from Australia with Satellite. This epic track features mesmerizing low end, eerie synth washes, distant hypnotic chants, loads of echoes & delays, and a stellar second drop, pure dancefloor obliteration.
