Californian’s 6Blocc & Dstro twist up a smasher featuring vocals from K’lin. Talk Too Loud has a sick relentless bass line and intricate technical programming, a massive tune to start off 2010 in big style.

For the flip side we introduce Bare with the track Culture. Bare has definite skills and comes to us highly recommended and recruited by 6blocc. Culture features classic reggae vibes, mental wobble, ragga vocals and deep bass.

DJ support from Boot, Dubtek, Roommate, The Abyss, Droid Sector and Fault.
