First is the unstoppable Smoke One, a fierce party tune that kills on the floor. Ragga vocals, sick synth leads & a rumbling bass line are all featured in this heavy stomper. Crucial.

For the flip side we introduce Nothing’s Final. This one’s deeper with intricate synths, relentless breaks along with a classic reggae styled bass line & serious wubs. Massive.

DJ support from Babylon System, Droid Sector, Dubtek, Boot, Fault, Roommate, & many others.
