Terminal Dusk welcomes Sarantis into the family and presents his Nitroglycerine / Half Dead 12″.
The Senseless Records owner from Athens, Greece now based in Leeds has been producing music for many years now. Whether alone or with accomplices, dubstep to dancehall, this Sarantis has done it all. Releases with Warrior Queen, Sasquatch & SNO on his own imprint Senseless as well as releases on Subsonik and Black Acre Records.

Terminal Dusk & Sarantis have chosen 2 very dark pieces to present herein. The A side explodes with Nitroglycerine, featuring Dialect & Asher Don. This track is fully realized & delivered over an entire side of glorious wax at a rapid 45 RPM. Sirens, dueling MC’s, sick low bass, razor sharp synth stabs & a thick intoxicating beat.

Flip to the B side and you get a truly demented rollercoaster of a tune called, Half Dead. Echos, delays, thug samples, extra bass, mental wobble, an excursion into street life. Not for the innocent.
