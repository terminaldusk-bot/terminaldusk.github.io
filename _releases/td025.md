Terminal Dusk delivers the goods once again with TD025 split Control on 12″ & digital.

This time around Australian’s Droid Sector breaks out a smooth techy roller on the A side with “Out Of Control”. Contagious dance floor action, trust.

Next up for the flip side, UK’s Nuphlo shows his skills with the extra busy, high octane dubstep smasher “Control Room”, guaranteed to set the floor on fire!

DJ support from 6blocc, Roommate, Dubtek, Boot, Sook, Requiem Audio, Fault & Komonazmuk to name a few.
