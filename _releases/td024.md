Next up in our split series is an extra weighty double A side platter featuring Dubtek & Boot.
The A side features Grief by Dubtek, a dance floor toaster with a monstrous rolling bass line, slick percussion & lush female vocals. Pure thunder.

For the AA side Boot steps up & delivers Boy. Solid bass, intricate programming, fleeting synth lines, an infectious groove & a blistering 2nd drop. Heavy!

Both tunes have received DJ support from 6blocc, Droid Sector, Requiem Audio, Fault, Komonazmuk, among many others.
