First up is Degrade with Talkin’ Greaze, a serious deep & dark one here.

The 2nd tune is Nuphlo with Xenophobia, bass loaded action, pure dance floor style, excellent vibes.

Flip side kicks off with Shockwave featuring the incredible Danny Ranks on vocal duties. Can’t get enough of this one here. Vibe! Finishing this release is Physical with the ferocious Teleport, just a plain monster of a tune, sick bass lines, catchy rave synths, guaranteed to fill the floor.

DJ support from N-Type, 6Blocc, Babylon System, Line 47, Dubtek, Droid Sector, Wheel and Deal Records and many many more..
