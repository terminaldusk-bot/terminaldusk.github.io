“Light” opens up the 12″ with esoteric keys and humming bass. Acidic blips and reverb drenched hits push you further into the brightness. Once you have almost reached complete nirvana the esoteric keys enter the realm once again to take you higher than ever before. The pulsing bass completes the transformation and leaves floating amongst the sub waves.

The B side takes you deep into the shadows with Komonazmuk as your guide. Ominous synth washes with a steady beat lead you into the darkness. Staggering bass and echoed synths creep in as you realize that all the light around you has vanished. Heavy bass drops bring you to the floor where the atmosphere is thick and there you will remain, stuck in the shadows.
