Terminal Dusk welcomes the return of Babylon System with the the heavyweight duo of Californian's Roommate & No Thing offer up another hefty platter.

Side A unveils "Regardless VIP". Anthem styled, extra solid bass, an infectious hook & a very clear message.
We remain the largest, we invade regardless! Punishing!

The flip side shows off a collaboration with Nibe called "Raclette". Huge bass lines, razor sharp wobble, horn militia, gunshots, rockin' !

DJ support on both tunes from The Truth, Von D., Argon, Droid Sector, Boot, Komonazmuk and Fault.
