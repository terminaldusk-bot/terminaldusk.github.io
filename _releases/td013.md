Terminal Dusk presents the 5th and final installment in our “color” series, Obsidian.

Kicking off the A side in fine style, LA’s mashup legend 6blocc teams up with Fiend for the sickest collaboration. Ill Technique features huge bass alongside serious jungle pressure. Next up, UK’s Physical delivers Synthetic, a deep rolling thrill ride complete with techy synths, piano leads and an ingenious wobble.

Over to the B side, Terminal Dusk’s very own Fault provides 2034, a sick wobbler with catchy synth leads, subtle amen breaks & rastafari vocals. Rounding out the 12″ is Australia’s Droid Sector with Abandoned, a roller complete with thunderous breaks & prominent epic bass, a very DJ friendly cut.
